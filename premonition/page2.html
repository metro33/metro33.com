The exhibition New York. Premonition was opened at the Independence Day, July 4, 2008.

After the official opening in the Museum of Modern Art, visitors moved to the left shore of Don river.
A one day summer camp for "Indians and cowboys" was based in the open air. Participants could chose the team, they liked and take part in the competitions.
